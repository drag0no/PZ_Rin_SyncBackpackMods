description=[h2] DESCRIPTION [/h2]
description=
description=Hate seeing a Duffel Bag with capacity of 38? Like... How is it even possible?
description=
description=This mod mainly made to address these issues, aligning and slightly buffing parameters of the backpacks across all popular mods.
description=
description=It doesn't add any new items, it just changes some parameters of existing bags and backpacks.
description=
description=Features:
description=- Sync Bags and Backpacks of the same type across multiple mods;
description=- Bigger capacity and weight reduction for many bags and backpacks;
description=- Speed penalty slightly reduced.
description=
description=BONUS:
description=- Reworked Satchels: you can wear a satchel with a backpack!
description=
